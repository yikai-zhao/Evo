How to Play

1. Move & Hunt
WASD to move. Left-click (or Space) to attack. Kill creatures to gain XP.
2. Defend & Dash
Right-click to defend (80% reduction + 30% reflect). X to dash through danger.
3. Collect Authorities
9 Authorities spawn on the map. Press 1, 2, or 3 to unleash screen-clearing powers.
4. Evolve & Ascend
Reach XP thresholds + complete quests to evolve through 9 mortal tiers and 10 divine sequences. Slay an Outer God to unlock apotheosis.
